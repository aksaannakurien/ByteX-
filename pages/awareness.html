🛡️ Cybersecurity Awareness

Cybersecurity awareness helps people understand online risks and follow safe practices to protect their personal information and digital identity.

🔑 Strong Password Practices

Strong passwords are the first layer of protection for your accounts.

• Use unique passwords
• Avoid sharing passwords
• Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

🎣 Avoid Phishing Attacks

Phishing tricks users into revealing sensitive information through fake messages, emails, or websites.

• Check suspicious links
• Verify unknown messages
• Never share OTPs

📱 Social Media Safety

Social media contains personal information that attackers may misuse.

• Manage privacy settings
• Avoid oversharing information
• Be careful with unknown accounts

💻 Device Security

Keeping devices secure helps prevent unauthorized access and malware infections.

• Update software regularly
• Use security tools
• Lock your devices

🌐 Safe Internet Practices

Safe browsing habits reduce the chance of becoming a victim of cyber attacks.

• Use HTTPS websites
• Avoid unsafe downloads
• Be careful on public Wi-Fi

🚨 After a Cyber Incident

If you experience a cyber incident, quick action can reduce damage.

• Change passwords
• Report suspicious activity
• Contact the service provider
